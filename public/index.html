How to Contribute

1. Select Media

Choose your best photos from class events, hangouts, or any moment worth remembering. We accept JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF formats.

2. Write Description

Provide context: who's in the photo, what was happening, and when it was taken. A good caption makes a memory last longer.

3. Send to Editor

Submit your upload and wait for admin verification. Once approved, your photo joins the archive for the entire class to see.
